## Guidelines

**Category Organization:**
- Group related technologies together
- Keep categories focused (5-8 technologies each)
- Leadership/soft skills can be separate category
- Order categories by relevance to your career focus

**Technology Specificity:**
- ✅ Specific: "Enterprise IdP", "EDR Solution (EDR)"
- ❌ Too vague: "Security tools", "Cloud platforms"
- Include version/variant if relevant: "Enterprise SaaS Platform (E3/E5)"

**Keep Updated:**
- Add new technologies as you learn them
- Don't remove old technologies (shows breadth)
- Mark proficiency level in resume if needed, not here
- Keep comprehensive - this is your full skillset

**Typical Categories for IT Leaders:**
1. Identity Management
2. Cloud Infrastructure & Platforms
3. Endpoint Management & Security
4. Automation & Development
5. Leadership & Operations
6. Service Delivery & Support
7. Networking & Infrastructure (if applicable)
8. Compliance & Governance (if applicable)

Customize categories to match your domain and experience level.
